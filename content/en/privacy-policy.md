### Tracking Technologies & Cookies
We totally use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash some info. We're talking beacons, tags, and scripts – all for collecting and tracking info to level up and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include: 🚀

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file plopped onto your Device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But heads up, if you don't accept Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say "no thanks" to Cookies, our Service might just be using them. Just sayin'.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and emails might have these tiny electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These let the Company, for instance, count who's visited those pages or opened an email. They also help with other website stats, like checking how popular a section is or making sure our systems and servers are all good. Pretty neat, huh? ✨

Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session." Persistent Cookies stick around on your computer or mobile device even after you've logged off, but Session Cookies get wiped the second you close your browser. Poof! 💨

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the reasons listed below: 👇

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super essential for giving you the services available on the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help verify who you are and stop any shady business with user accounts. Without these, the services you're asking for just wouldn't happen, and we only use 'em for that purpose. No funny stuff! 😉

- **Cookies Policy / Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are basically our way of checking if you've given the green light to use Cookies on the Website. Simple as that! ✅

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are awesome because they let us remember your choices when you're using the Website – like your login deets or language preference. Their whole point is to give you a super personalized experience, so you don't have to re-enter your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet! 🤩

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and your choices? Hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy. We've got all the deets there. 🤓
